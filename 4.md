# 🚀 Upwork Notes — Part 3

## (Quality Bidding & Training the Algorithm)

---

# 1. Quality Over Quantity

❌ Wrong:

```text id="3mchfe"
Apply to 50 jobs every day.
```

✅ Correct:

```text id="e9e1uj"
Apply to 3-5 highly relevant jobs.
```

Remember:

```text id="5e2d75"
Reduce number of bids.
Increase quality of bids.
```

---

# 2. Upwork AI Algorithm Is Watching

Think:

```text id="d3m6cr"
The food belongs to someone else,
but the stomach is yours.
```

(Urdu example from the video)

Meaning:

```text id="k4xqjg"
The job belongs to the client,
but your Upwork profile and
algorithm reputation belong to you.
```

---

# 3. Invites Become Free But Algorithm Is Yours

Example:

```text id="kv4r6h"
Client sends invite.
```

You may think:

```text id="ljj5m9"
Free invite.
Let's apply.
```

Wrong.

Because:

```text id="94l2tl"
Every action trains your algorithm.
```

---

# 4. Build Your Own Upwork Structure

Question:

```text id="1ms5yi"
How does Upwork identify me?
```

You want Upwork to think:

```text id="6gmnq8"
This person is an AWS + Terraform
+ DevOps specialist.
```

Not:

```text id="0xydc0"
AWS
Azure
Python
WordPress
Graphic Design
Data Entry
AI
```

---

# 5. Don't Do Random Work

Guru's statement:

```text id="gib3wa"
Yaar bhai, ganda kaam nahi karna.
```

Meaning:

Don't accept jobs that:

* damage your niche
* waste your time
* confuse the algorithm

---

✅

# 7. Before Applying Ask:

```text id="zq95b6"
Who is this client?
```

Always check:

### Client spending

Example:

```text id="j0x5z5"
$100K spent
```

Good signal.

---

### Interview history

Example:

```text id="lmsuzr"
69 interviews
0 hires
```

Red flag.

---

### Payment verification

Check:

```text id="7op72o"
Payment verified?
```

---

### Hiring history

Ask:

```text id="nght7u"
Does this client actually hire?
```

---

# 8. Learn To Say No

Not every job deserves a proposal.

Example:

```text id="gvcghu"
Need Senior Kubernetes Architect
15 years experience
$20 budget
```

Answer:

```text id="wpf4ia"
No.
```

---

# 9. Declining Helps Train The Algorithm

Don't ignore bad invites.

Instead:

```text id="rvfwul"
Decline invite
+
Select reason.
```

This teaches Upwork:

```text id="iq9nha"
Show Bilal more AWS jobs.
Show fewer unrelated jobs.
```

---

# 10. Time Is Your Biggest Asset

Question:

```text id="az12r8"
Will this project help me
grow my career?
```

If not:

```text id="d7a1ng"
Don't do it.
```

---

# 11. One Sentence To Remember

```text id="a91kqj"
You are not applying for jobs.

You are training the Upwork algorithm
to understand who you are.
```

# Bilal's Upwork Checklist Before Applying

```text id="0drl0o"
✓ Can I do this job?

✓ Is this my niche?

✓ Who is the client?

✓ Does the client hire?

✓ What is the client's pain point?

✓ Will this improve my profile?

✓ Will this improve the algorithm?
```
