**How to Find Specific Agencies on Upwork?**

Because you want to get inspiration for an agency structure, the best approach is to use Upwork’s internal search system:
- Log into your Upwork account.
- Go to the search bar and switch the dropdown filter from "Jobs" to "Talent".
- Type keywords like AWS DevOps or Cloud Infrastructure.
- Use the left-hand filters to check the box for "Agencies" under the talent type.

This will hide individual freelancers and display only active agencies so you can study their taglines, portfolios, and pricing structures.

Bilal, aapne bilkul point ki baat pakri hai! Upwork par kamyabi ka sab se bara raaz yahi hai ke proposal bhejte waqt aap do chashmon (lenses) se dekhein: **About the Client** aur **Activity on this Job**.

Chalein isko bilkul simple aur standard rulebook mein convert karte hain taake aap aage se sirf 10 seconds mein decision le sakein:

---

## 1. About the Client (Kya Client Serious Hai?)

Yahan aap client ki "auqaat" aur uski past history check karte hain.

* **Payment Method Verified:** Yeh hamesha tick hona chahiye. Unverified par risk 90% barh jata hai.
* **Hire Rate:** Yeh hamesha **50% se upar** hona chahiye. Is client ka 20% hai, yani yeh 20 jobs post karta hai toh sirf 4 logon ko hire karta hai—baqi 16 jobs par log connects zaya karte hain.
* **Total Spent & Avg Hourly Rate Paid:** Yeh aapki agency (*WHBrowns*) ki pricing strategy ke liye zaroori hai. Agar client ka total spent kam hai (jaise yahan $905 hai) aur avg hourly rate sirf **$3.00/hr** hai, toh iska matlab hai yeh chote aur saste kaam karwane wala client hai. Yeh aapko engineering value ke paise nahi dega.

---

## 2. Activity on this Job (Kya Abhi Chance Hai?)

Yahan aap check karte hain ke kya is job par apply karne ka koi faida bacha bhi hai ya train nikal chuki hai.

* **Proposals (20 to 50):** Agar 50+ ho chuke hon aur job 2-3 din purani ho, toh chance kam hota hai.
* **Last Viewed by Client:** Yeh sab se critical hai. Agar yahan "last viewed: **last week**" likha hai, iska matlab hai client job post karke bhool chuka hai, wo active nahi hai. Hamein un jobs par apply karna hai jahan "last viewed: *minutes ago*" ya *hours ago* ho.
* **Hires (1) & Interviewing (2):** Agar client pehle hi kisi ko hire kar chuka hai (*Hires: 1*) aur mazeed logon ka interview le raha hai, toh naye proposal bhejte hi aapka number sab se aakhri qatar (queue) mein aayega.

---

## Your New Golden Rule for WHBrowns 🚀

Agli baar jab aap koi AWS ya Terraform ki job dekhein, toh us par apply sirf tabhi karein jab:

1. **Hire Rate** 45%-50% se zyada ho.
2. **Payment Method** Verified ho.
3. **Hires** 0 hon (yani abhi kisi ko rakha na ho).
4. **Last Viewed** "today" ya "hours ago" ho.

Is check-list ko yaad rakhna aapke connects bhi bachayega aur aapko direct high-converting responses dilwayega!
